How It Works

  1. Easy Connection When you're within range of an InterPay Wi-Fi hotspot, simply tap on the InterPay Wi-Fi from the list of available connections detected by your device.
  2. Seamless Redirection You'll receive a notification that redirects you to our InterPay website.
  3. Select a Package Browse through our range of packages and choose the one that best fits your needs.
  4. Secure Payment Enter your Safaricom number when prompted, and proceed with the payment through our secure transaction system.
  5. Instant Access After confirming your payment with your PIN, you'll be connected to the InterPay Wi-Fi until your chosen package expires.
